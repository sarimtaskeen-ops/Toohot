Our story

Fast food, done with fire.

Too Hot Too Spicy started with one idea: fast food doesn't have to be forgettable. In Shahdara Town, Lahore, we cook every broast, burger and pizza with a heavier hand on flavour and a shorter wait between your order and your table.

Whether you're stopping in for dine-in, grabbing a takeaway box, pulling up for curbside pickup, or waiting on a no-contact delivery, the goal stays the same — hot, fresh, and worth the drive.
